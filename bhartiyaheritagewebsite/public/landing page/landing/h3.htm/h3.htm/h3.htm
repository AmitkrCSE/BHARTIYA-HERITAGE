Bhagta Parab

A tribal festival marking spring and agricultural renewal in Jharkhand.

Chhath Puja

An ancient Hindu festival dedicated to the Sun God, celebrated with devotion.

Karma Puja

A harvest festival celebrated by various tribal communities, symbolizing nature's bounty.

Diwali

The festival of lights, symbolizing the victory of good over evil and the arrival of prosperity.

Holi

The festival of colors, celebrating the arrival of spring and the triumph of good.

Navaratri

Nine nights dedicated to Goddess Durga, celebrating her divine power.
